Raw data

资料来源与数据口径

完整原始资料库 github.com/zihaomu/dont-learn-buffett/tree/main/raw_data

1957-1964 Buffett line uses partnership gain before partner allocation.

1965-2024 Buffett line uses Berkshire per-share market value from the 2024 annual report.

S&P 500 and Dow comparison lines use price-index year-end closes from raw daily CSV files.

Berkshire's official S&P 500 total-return comparison is also preserved for 1965-2024 in processed/returns.csv.

限制

  • Shareholder-letter PDFs are consolidated under primary/warren_buffett_letters/; duplicate upstream PDF folders are not retained locally.
  • Dow and S&P price-index series exclude dividends. Use sp500_total_return_from_berkshire_pct for the official 1965-2024 total-return benchmark.
  • 唐朝/唐书房 source collection is represented by public context pages and book descriptions, not full WeChat article archives.

来源

  • Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters index primary

    Official index for shareholder letters. Local letter PDFs are consolidated in the public raw archive instead of duplicate source folders.

  • Berkshire Hathaway 2024 Annual Report primary

    Source for 1965-2024 Berkshire vs. S&P 500 annual percentage table.

    Local: primary/berkshire_2024_annual_report.pdf
  • Warren Buffett letters raw archive primary

    Canonical public raw archive. Includes one PDF per year from 1957-2024 plus early partnership H1/midyear letters.

    Local: primary/warren_buffett_letters/
  • Buffett Partnership Letters PDF source_archive_reference

    Upstream reference for early partnership letters; canonical local copies live under primary/warren_buffett_letters/.

  • Warren Buffett shareholder letters 1957-2018 English PDF archive source_archive_reference

    Upstream archive used to build the canonical local by-year archive; duplicate source PDFs are not retained locally.

  • Warren Buffett letters to partners 1959-1975 index primary_index

    Useful index for early partner/Berkshire letters not hosted as current official PDFs.

  • Historical time series of Dow Jones Industrial Average and S&P 500 market_data

    Raw daily CSV files used to compute year-end price-index returns.

    Local: market_indices_raw/
  • Britannica Money: Warren Buffett biography secondary

    Secondary chronology and biographical context.

  • Britannica Money: Berkshire Hathaway overview secondary

    Secondary context on Berkshire's business mix.

  • Novel Investor notes on Buffett Partnership Letters secondary

    Secondary summary of partnership period and Dow comparison.

  • 唐书房 / 巴芒演义 context secondary_chinese

    Chinese context for 唐朝's Buffett/Munger writing series and 巴芒演义.

  • 微信读书: 唐朝作品介绍 secondary_chinese

    Chinese source for 唐朝's value-investing books, including 巴芒演义.
